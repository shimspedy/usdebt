Data Methodology

Live Updates: Debt and cash balance use daily Treasury data; receipts/outlays use monthly Treasury statements; population & GDP use annual World Bank data. "Live" counters interpolate smoothly between the most recent observations using linear progression for levels and continuous compounding for growth series.

Derived Metrics: Per-citizen values and ratios are computed in real-time from the live base values, providing current estimates that update continuously between official data releases.
